Summary: Lattitude vs X

The original purpose of this project was to get a good grade on Homework 6 in the Data Science Bootcamp...

Initial questions and findings:


What's the weather like as we approach the equator? Now, we know what you may be thinking: "Duh. It gets hotter..."

But, if pressed, how would you prove it?


Charts created:

1. City Latitude vs. Max Temperature:

- there is a slight correlation - being closer to the equator does result in higher temperatures

2. City Latitude vs. Humidity:

- no significant relationship between the two

- many cities have high humidity (close to the sea?)

3. City Latitude vs. Cloudiness:

- no significant relationship between the two

- cloudiness seems to be assigned in an arbitrary fashion (10,20, 40, etc.)

4. City Latitude vs. Wind Speed:

- very little range in wind speed

- no significant relationship between the two
