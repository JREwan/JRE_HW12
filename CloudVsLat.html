Clouds vs. Latitude


Cloud coverage compared to the latitiude of a chosen city.



After extensive study of the City Latitude vs. Cloudiness, it was determined that there was no significant relationship between the two

This might have been caused by the arbitrary way the "cloudiness" percentage was assigned. Cloudiness seems to be assigned in an arbitrary fashion (10,20, 40, etc.)

Without a scientific measeurement system, 10 percent cloudy in Phoenix might be very different from 10 percent cloudy in Glasgow
